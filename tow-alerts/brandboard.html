Name & Logo Exploration

Six directions for Autura's meeting-intelligence app. Same palette, six different lenses. All type is real and embedded — this is exactly how each mark renders.
Lens 1 · the incumbent, refined
ToWatch
tow + watch. Your original, with your gradient idea: it opens in deep Autura blue and exhales to slate as the word ends — the triangle stays on the T. Honest, descriptive, zero explanation needed.
safe · descriptive
Lens 2 · the metaphor
undertow
tow, hiding inside a real word. The undertow is the current beneath the surface — exactly what this app reads: what city councils are pulling toward before it surfaces as an RFP. Editorial serif, depth-gradient like water. The most memorable name in the set.
flavorful · evocative
Lens 3 · the forecast
Towcast
tow + forecast. Says the product's whole promise in one word: it tells you the weather before it arrives — a broadcast beacon with the Autura triangle as the transmitter. Modern grotesk, tech-forward, very "official product."
product-ready · forward-looking
Lens 4 · the sales rep
TowHold
toehold, respelled. A toehold is the small early position that wins you the whole climb — which is literally what a BD rep gets from early intel. Heavy slab with a blue shadow: gritty, confident, a little swagger.
bold · bd-minded
Lens 5 · the wire service
TOWLINE
towline: the rope that does the pulling — and a news wire. Monospace, spaced like a teletype header; the underline is the line itself, curling up like a hook at the end. Feels like intelligence briefings, because it is one.
technical · insider
Lens 6 · early warning
TowRadar
says exactly what it does. Condensed caps like an instrument panel, live sweep, and one red blip — the deck's red accent finally has a job: it's the alert. The most literal name; nobody will ever ask what the app is for.
literal · ops-flavored
My read: Towcast is the best "official company app" name — one real-feeling word, states the forward-looking promise, safe in front of any boss or agency. Undertow is the one people will remember and talk about — pick it if you want flavor over description. ToWatch remains a solid bronze: the pun works, it just describes watching rather than predicting. TowHold is the rep's inside joke, Towline the analyst's, TowRadar the operator's. Any of these takes ~2 minutes to apply to the live dashboard.
